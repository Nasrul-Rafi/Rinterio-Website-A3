Frequently Asked Questions


(Here are some answers of questions that we are usually asked)


Why should I buy my plans from you?

You can achieve cost savings of up to 90% over custom house plans. It may take hundreds of hours and thousands of dollars to design a home from scratch. A ready-made plan saves you time and money - and can be modified to suit your needs.Many pre-drawn plans have been previously built, and most have had the construction problems resolved. This translates into far fewer design headaches and reduced labor costs for you at the time of building your plan.

How do you fix the budget?

The cost will vary based on location, materials, and labor.Clearing the land, leveling, and any necessary demolition or excavation work also adds to the cost.Paying for the design, structural plans, and blueprints can range from 5% to 15% of the total construction cost.Items like kitchen cabinets, bathroom fixtures, lighting, and appliances need to be budgeted for. The cost varies depending on the quality and brand you choose.

How much will this house cost to build?

House construction costs are dependent on where the house is built, the level of finish (tile, trim, window quality, etc...) and the site.We can provide a “cost to build” estimate by looking and examining the land.To get an accurate estimate, purchase a set of plans and submit them to builders for bids.

What is a materials list and what does it include?

A material list is a list of building supplies needed to construct the infrastructure of new house. This includes materials such as insulation, concrete, and hardware. However, it usually doesn't include lighting, thermoscale, AC etc.
